Developed by Tom Daly, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Casual, Indie, Simulation genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Little Aviary is a cozy idle bird collector that sits at the bottom of your screen! Hatch unique birds, sell them, decorate their home, then watch them fly around your screen and perch on top of your other windows!
