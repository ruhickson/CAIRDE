Documented Title: Chimeral Fantasy

Developed by Leanne Rath, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the RPG genre, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Adventure through a whimsical fantasy world, assemble a party of quirky characters you meet along the way, and fight monsters in turn based tactical combat!
