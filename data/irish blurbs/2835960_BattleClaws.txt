Developed by DameGev, EveryPlanet, Gronkiee, and endoran and published by DameGev, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Casual, Early Access genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, BattleClaws garnered 7 user reviews critical reception, accumulating 7 player reviews. Grab, drop, and score in the ultimate claw machine showdown! BattleClaws is a fast-paced, arcade-style party game for 2–4 players. Take control of a claw, snatch the right prizes, and outscore your friends in chaotic, rivalry inducing gameplay. Features:Couch Chaos: Up to 4 players battle it out locally in quick, competitive matches.Quick Fire objectives: The target item changes mid-round, so move quick!Clawsmetics:  Customize your claw with hats, faces, and arcade-inspired accessories.Tiebreaker Mode: Sudden death overtime where only the fastest claw survives.
