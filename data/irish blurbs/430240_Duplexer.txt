Documented Title: Duplexer (Released: 2016)

Developed by Vantage Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Indie genre, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2010s.

Upon release, Duplexer garnered mixed critical reception, accumulating 14 player reviews. Duplexer is a challenging single-player puzzle platformer game. The player can push or pull the walls, floor and ceiling around them to overcome many puzzling, platforming and physics based trials.The game is designed to encourage players to solve puzzles in new and innovative ways. By focusing on a very simple mechanic, the game opens up near-endless level possibilities and puzzle solving methods.
