Documented Title: Guild of Dungeoneering Ultimate Edition (Released: 2015)

Developed by Gambrinous, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Indie, RPG genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2010s.

Upon release, Guild of Dungeoneering Ultimate Edition garnered mostly positive critical reception, accumulating 2,606 player reviews. Join Gambrinous on DiscordAbout the GameNow fully rebuilt and remastered, Guild of Dungeoneering Ultimate Edition is a turn-based dungeon crawler with a twist: instead of controlling the hero you build the dungeon around them. Using cards drawn from your Guild decks, you lay down rooms, monsters, traps and of course loot! Meanwhile your hero is making their own decisions on where to go and what to fight. But will they be strong enough to take on the deepest dungeons? In between dungeon runs you manage your Guild, building new rooms to attract new classes of adventurer and expand your decks ...
