Documented Title: Ravel the Frays

Developed by Margarita Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Casual, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Ravel the Frays is a sci-fi mystery puzzle game set aboard a generation ship drifting through space. AILISH is the computer who runs the ship and predicts all future timelines to steer it. However she now foresees an unavoidable fate no matter what timeline she follows.
