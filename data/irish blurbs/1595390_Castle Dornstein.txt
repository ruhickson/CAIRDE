Documented Title: Castle Dornstein

Developed by Digital Mosaic Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Hemlock is a young scholar invited to a crumbling castle in the Bohemian countryside. The wealthy nobleman Lord Fiodor Dornstein has hired him to translate a mysterious medieval book known as The Devil's Diary, a text rumored to harbor an ancient evil.But when the book goes missing on Hemlock's first night, everyone becomes a suspect. In order to find the book, Hemlock will need to explore the castle grounds, question the local villagers, and unravel the secrets of a dark past.
