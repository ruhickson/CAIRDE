Documented Title: Tax-Force

Developed by Outsider Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Hunt down tax-evading billionaires and rebuild society in a 2D action-roguelite platformer inspired by 90's comicbooks. You’ll fight through mutant henchmen with melee focused souls-lite combat, make tough moral choices, rebuild society and upgrade your biomes with a unique card-collecting economy system, and discover a time-looping branching narrative. Your goal; negotiate repayments from your targets to repay the planet’s Solar Shield debt, preventing planetwide destruction.
