Documented Title: Junk Sec

Developed by Shoehead, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action genre, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Abigail is the youngest member of JunkSec, a salvage company specializing in recovering objects and artifacts from the ruins of the Old City, destroyed 10 years ago when an apparition appeared in the skies above it.
