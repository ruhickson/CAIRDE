Documented Title: Exercise your Demons

Developed by BoopDood, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Step into the world of Exercise your Demons, a short 2d narrative adventure inspired by Irish fairies and folklore. You will take the role of a character who is struggling to find a job and finds some reprieve thanks to your uncle's friend who gives you a chance.Meet and befriend 5 locals while you work towards settling into the small fictional town called Inishsióg in County Galway, Ireland. The locals are not normal people but who is to say what is normal. The locals are known as tricksters and sometimes even bad omens.
