The best cop in town has been murdered, and there's only one man who can solve the case: Sergeant Squidley, Space Cop!Hastily promoted from traffic duty, Squidley must use all his Strength, Investigation, and Handiness as he travels around Moontropolis searching for evidence to solve the case. But there are stronger forces than just the criminal underworld at play: an ex-wife who hates him, a young son to take care of, and a dark secret held by pretty much everyone in town. A decision-based RPG that lets you steer the story as you wish! Want to be a dutiful police officer? Or maybe you want...
