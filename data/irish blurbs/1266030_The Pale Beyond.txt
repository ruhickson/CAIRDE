Documented Title: The Pale Beyond (Released: 2023)

Developed by Saltstone Studios and published by Fellow Traveller, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Indie, RPG, Simulation genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, The Pale Beyond garnered very positive critical reception, accumulating 1,999 player reviews. Five years ago, The Viscount and its crew set sail through The Pale Passage in search of absolute magnetic South. The ship and its crew were tragically lost to the ice, never to be seen again. Her sister ship, The Temperance shall follow in her path, hoping to find answers where The Viscount failed.A polar exploration game set in an unforgiving frozen wasteland where you embark on a treacherous voyage across the ice and through the Pale Beyond.
