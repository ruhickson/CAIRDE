Documented Title: Supermarket Shriek (Released: 2020)

Developed by Billy Goat Entertainment and published by PQube, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Casual, Indie, Racing genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, Supermarket Shriek garnered positive critical reception, accumulating 40 player reviews. Join man and goat in this chaotic, award-winning couch-co-op adventure! Tackle outlandish obstacle courses, complete rewarding challenges and dodge deadly obstacles in a hectic race to the checkout! Packed with a frantic 38 stage campaign and 4 hilariously fun PvP party modes for 2-8 players. Scream like a man! Scream like a goat! Scream together!
