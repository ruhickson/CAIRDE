Documented Title: Inertial Drift (Released: 2020)

Developed by Level 91 Entertainment and published by PQube, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Indie, Racing genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, Inertial Drift garnered very positive critical reception, accumulating 1,550 player reviews. Set in a 90's retro future, Inertial Drift is an Arcade Racer with a devastatingly unique handling model and unbelievably satisfying twin-stick drift mechanics. Inertial Drift offers a whole new level of accessibility and challenge to the arcade racing genre - for a driving experience genuinely unlike anything you've played before.The left stick is used for steering, while the right hand Drift Stick gives you independent control over your drift.
