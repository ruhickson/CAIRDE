Documented Title: Jennifer Wilde: Unlikely Revolutionaries (Released: 2022)

Developed by Outsider Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, Jennifer Wilde: Unlikely Revolutionaries garnered positive critical reception, accumulating 26 player reviews. At the start of the Jazz Age in Paris in 1921, young French artist Jennifer Chevalier becomes embroiled in death, espionage and revolution which takes her across France, England and Ireland with the ghost of Oscar Wilde. A Point &amp; Click 2D adventure with a unique comic book inventory design.Jennifer believes the recent death of her father was no 'accident'. Finding a chained locket in her father's hotel, she attempts to use her childhood gift for talking to ghosts to call his spirit back.
