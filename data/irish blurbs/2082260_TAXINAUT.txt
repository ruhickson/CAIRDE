Documented Title: TAXINAUT (Released: 2022)

Developed by mosgrom, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Indie, RPG, Simulation genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, TAXINAUT garnered positive critical reception, accumulating 24 player reviews. Explore the Kyprei-41 galaxy as a lonely TAXINAUT carrying fares across alien worlds. Descent onto planets and fly over alien cities and landscapes. Land at bars, diners, garages and many other common and exotic locations. Interact with the locals to ask for directions or learn about the many esoteric and often clandestine aspects of the Kyprei-41 galaxy.FEATURESRPG and space taxi sim / life sim in one: Lead the life of a working-class astronaut, pilot a spaceship and transport passengers across an open-world galaxy.Fly both on planets and in space.
