Documented Title: LazerGrrl (Released: 2024)

Developed by Sandwich Generation, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action, Indie, Strategy, Free To Play genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, LazerGrrl garnered positive critical reception, accumulating 25 player reviews. LazerGrrl is a fast-paced PVP strategy game. It combines the fury of Bomberman with the strategic depth of an RTS. Find the right balance between building your own base and blasting your opponent's.
