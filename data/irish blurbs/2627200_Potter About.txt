Documented Title: Potter About

Developed by Stableboy Entertainment, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Casual, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Potter About is a traditional, 2D side scrolling platformer with mesmerizing, hand-drawn graphics and a cozy, calming vibe. This relaxing, short but sweet journey follows a well-worn plant pot that wakes up alone and abandoned in a trash bin. Not ready to forget its old life, it decides to embark on an adventure.Explore a charming, cluttered house filled with surprises, gain magical, plant-inspired abilities, and use them to overcome any obstacles that stand in your way. It's a fun, calming adventure for players of all ages.
