Documented Title: The Ghost of Redstone Manor (Released: 2024)

Developed by Bashevis Games and published by Bashevis, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

The year is 1950, and you're Patricia Rotstein, the esteemed mystery novelist. Or well, you were, before your untimely death.  Now, with the help of only your cat, Hexe, and some newfound ghostly abilities, you must investigate your own murder and mend the myriad problems your family members face before it's too late. You'll need to be ready for puzzles, possessions, and more.This game was created originally as part of Trinity College Dublin's Interactive Digital Media MSC program, 2022.
