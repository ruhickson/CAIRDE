Documented Title: Buildings Have Feelings Too! (Released: 2021)

Developed by Blackstaff Games and published by Silver Lining Interactive and Maple Whispering Limited, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Indie, Simulation, Strategy genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.
