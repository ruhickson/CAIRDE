Documented Title: Elemental Exiles (Released: 2024)

Developed by Final Game Studio, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Indie, RPG, Strategy genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, Elemental Exiles garnered mostly positive critical reception, accumulating 18 player reviews. Follow a cast of unique characters in this Deck Building Roguelike, expanding your arsenal of abilities to overcome any challenges that stand in the way of your chosen hero's personal quest as you explore the world, adapting to new twists on every playthrough.Play as one of 12 unique characters scattered across the map, each with a diverse deck of abilities at their disposal.  Guide them carefully across the world to advance their own personal quest and help them achieve glory - or at least closure. 4 Characters are unlocked from the start, but there are 8 more hidden out there in the world...
