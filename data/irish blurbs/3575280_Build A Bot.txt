Documented Title: Build A Bot

Developed by DESKRAGE, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action, Casual, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Fight! And Fight Again!Taking down the corporation is a big task, so if you fail, don’t worry, Wheat Willy has made plenty of spare mechs to reupload your AI to. With each attempt, you’ll pick up new skills and learn new things about the various enemies and gear along the path to victory. Chew your way through hordes of machines as you forge ahead towards the headquarters to finish this once and for all, no matter how much scrap you must make on your way. Build Your BotDesign your perfect killing machine as you pick between randomly dropped weapons, parts and upgrades throughout your run.
