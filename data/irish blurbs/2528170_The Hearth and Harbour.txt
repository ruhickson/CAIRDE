Documented Title: The Hearth and Harbour

Developed by Saltstone Studios and published by Fellow Traveller, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, RPG, Simulation genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

You have left your life and home country behind, travelling north across the sea in pursuit of your dreams and the hope of avoiding the shadow of an impending war.And so, you find yourself in the harbour city of Lewthport, the proud owner of ‘The Silver Spoon’, a run-down restaurant in a run-down part of town. Now it’s up to you to make your dream a reality. With a hefty loan to repay, you’ll need to improve the restaurant quickly… expand the menu, upgrade the decor, build connections with suppliers and keep your customers happy.Meanwhile, a storm is brewing outside.
