Documented Title: Prometheus Wept (Released: 2025)

Developed by Timeslip Softworks, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Indie, RPG, Strategy, Early Access genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, Prometheus Wept garnered positive critical reception, accumulating 12 player reviews. Prometheus Wept is a turn-based, party-based isometric role playing game set on Earth, in the aftermath of the digital apocalypse. Imagine a near-future Earth where technology has become even more integrated and essential to daily life. Imagine the sudden destruction of all our digital technology, in less than a day, by an unprecedented cyber-attack. The virus tears apart the global web of largely automated agriculture, production and transport.
