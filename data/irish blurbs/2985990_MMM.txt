Documented Title: MMM (Released: 2025)

Developed by We Are Dinosaurs, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action, Adventure, Indie, Strategy genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

You. In a Maze. With a T-Rex.Get to the exit before Rex gets to you!Inspired by the 1980's classic 3D Monster Maze - arguably the first ever horror game - MMM recreates the horror and panic of knowing you're a heartbeat away from becoming Rex's next meal.
