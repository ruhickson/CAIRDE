Documented Title: Factorio (Released: 2020)

Developed by Wube Software LTD., this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Casual, Indie, Simulation, Strategy genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, Factorio garnered overwhelmingly positive critical reception, accumulating 218,289 player reviews. Factorio is a game in which you build and maintain factories. You will be mining resources, researching technologies, building infrastructure, automating production and fighting enemies. In the beginning you will find yourself chopping trees, mining ores and crafting mechanical arms and transport belts by hand, but in short time you can become an industrial powerhouse, with huge solar fields, oil refining and cracking, manufacture and deployment of construction and logistic robots, all for your resource needs.
