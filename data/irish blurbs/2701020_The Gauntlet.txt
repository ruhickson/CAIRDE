Documented Title: The Gauntlet (Released: 2024)

Developed by Round Table Studios and published by Scattergood Studios, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action, Casual, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, The Gauntlet garnered positive critical reception, accumulating 14 player reviews. The Gauntlet is a single player, parkour like speed-runner game. You play as a contestant in a new Game Show hosted by the evil master-mind known as Bad Bat. Race through a multitude of lethal obstacle courses all of which have been designed with your demise in mind. If you can make it through Bad Bat’s chambers the fastest, you can leave with the Grand Prize! Your Life.
