Documented Title: Brass & Bramble

Developed by Puca Labs, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, RPG genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Brass &amp; Bramble is a narrative deck-builder set in an alternate steampunk Victorian age where myth and empire collide. Step into the role of Avalon’s Envoy, wielding your deck to battle the British Empire’s brass-forged legions across the globe, grow your bonds with mythological legends, and rebuild Avalon as your home base. Build your deck, choose your companions, and decide the future of Avalon.Tactical card combat interwoven with story encounters.
