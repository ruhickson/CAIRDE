Documented Title: Swing Boy

Developed by Robert O'Connor and published by Yellowbyte Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Swing Boy is a precision platformer that takes swinging to a new level. Use your grappling hook to navigate your way out of the underground and back to the surface. What has changed since you were gone? What is left?Escaping to the surface is no walk in the park! You'll have to swing through toxic sewers, navigate abandoned underground labs &amp; avoid dangerous enemies.
