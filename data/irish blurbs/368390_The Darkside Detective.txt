Documented Title: The Darkside Detective (Released: 2017)

Developed by Spooky Doorway and published by Akupara Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Casual, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2010s.
