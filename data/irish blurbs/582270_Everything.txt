Documented Title: Everything (Released: 2017)

Developed by David OReilly and published by David OReilly and PLAYISM (Japan), this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Casual, Indie, RPG, Simulation genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2010s.

Upon release, Everything garnered very positive critical reception, accumulating 3,780 player reviews. Everything is an interactive experience where every object in the Universe is a playable character - from animals to planets to galaxies and beyond. Travel between outer and inner space, and explore a vast, interconnected universe of things without enforced goals, scores, or tasks to complete.
