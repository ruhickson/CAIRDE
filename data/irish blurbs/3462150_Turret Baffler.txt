Documented Title: Turret Baffler (Released: 2025)

Developed by Aceade Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action genre, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

In the seedy underbelly of Bailecittà, the line between thief and private investigator is as clear as the fog that blankets the streets and factories.You are Sadmuc, a freelance “Baffler”. Few of the electronic surveillance systems and safeguards throughout this city can stand up to your custom EMP railgun. But shooting them from a distance doesn’t get you past them. Your entire job is to get somebody else past those hazards.And when it comes to sniffing around the Borflaw corporation's facilities in the sealed-off Bonglesticks area, the only person you can rely on for that is your old part...
