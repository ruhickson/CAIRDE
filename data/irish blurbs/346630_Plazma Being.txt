Documented Title: Plazma Being (Released: 2015)

Developed by Hedge Knight Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action, Adventure, Indie genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2010s.

Upon release, Plazma Being garnered mostly positive critical reception, accumulating 399 player reviews. Take control of Zeb in this puzzle/platformer. He is a small energy based life form from space, known as a Plazma Being. While journeying through space to his home, Zeb was surprised to find that he was being kidnapped by aliens. They took him back to their planet and unceremoniously threw him into captivity. During this time a series of earthquakes shook the planet and wreaked havoc upon the alien civilization, but gave Zeb a chance to escape.
