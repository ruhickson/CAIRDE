Documented Title: DeTails

Developed by Can You Eat It?, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Adventure, Casual, Indie, Racing genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

DeTails is a punk-powered adventure about The HEXES as they travel around Long Den on their first ever tour. But something strange is happening at the location of each venue, colourless voids are disrupting normal life and someone has been kidnapping the local mole population.Dive into a vibrant world of rebellion, freedom, friendship and growing up without losing your edge.Explore Long Den, a visually stunning 2D/3D world oozing with characterMeet a whole host of local weirdos!Use colour to change the environment and solve puzzles or just ruin someones day!Avoid the pigs and make your mark...
