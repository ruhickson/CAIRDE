Documented Title: Fighters' Glory (Released: 2020)

Developed by Corbally Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action genre, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Fighters' Glory is a 1v1 weapons based fighting game. 
Ten warriors face off in a tournament to win fortune and glory!
Features include full 3d movement and  local or online Multiplayer.
