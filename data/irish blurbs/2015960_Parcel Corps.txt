Documented Title: Parcel Corps (Released: 2024)

Developed by Billy Goat Entertainment Ltd and published by Secret Mode, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action, Adventure, Indie, Racing genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, Parcel Corps garnered mixed critical reception, accumulating 101 player reviews.
