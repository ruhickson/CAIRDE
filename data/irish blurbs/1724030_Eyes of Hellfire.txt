Documented Title: Eyes of Hellfire (Released: 2025)

Developed by Gambrinous and published by Blumhouse Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the RPG, Strategy, Early Access genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during the 2020s.

Upon release, Eyes of Hellfire garnered mostly positive critical reception, accumulating 176 player reviews. Step into the shadowed halls of the Hellfire Club, where something, or someone, is always lying... Eyes of Hellfire is a richly atmospheric gothic horror cooperative game that plunges you and up to 4 of your friends into a cursed lodge. The game is based on real Irish legends of the secret society known as the Hellfire Club, resulting in a scenario filled with secrets, monsters, and a creeping sense of dread.Each player harbours a hidden curse, twisting their abilities and motives, as they explore the ever-shifting rooms of the lodge.
