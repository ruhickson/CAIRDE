Documented Title: Necromage

Developed by CrazyPepe Games, this work represents a contribution to Ireland's digital game development heritage. Classified within the Action, Indie, RPG genres, this title demonstrates the technical and creative capabilities of Irish game development studios during this period.

Necromage lets you summon a skeleton army to fight and explore a vast, corrupted world. Rise from the underworld to restore what your kind once plunged into chaos. Hunt down and confront your greatest enemy.Key Features:Master the art of summoning creatures from all corners of the world.Craft powerful spells to level up and enhance your creatures' gear.Embark on an epic journey through diverse realms: start in the underworld and ascend to the upper world.Uncover and conquer challenging dungeons. Level up and equip your main character with the finest gear.
